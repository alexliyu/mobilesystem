_extra_from_distance

_extra_from_distance(filter, submission_id_column)

This uses the Spherical Law of Cosines for a close enough approximation of distances. distance = acos(sin(lat1) * sin(lat2) + cos(lat1) * cos(lat2) * cos(lng2 - lng1)) * 3959 The "radius" of the earth varies between 3,950 and 3,963 miles.
